FAQ

What actually happens if I cancel?

When you cancel, your hosting is terminated the following billing cycle. Any domain names registered by us can be transferred to you. We aim to keep you as a client because of our value, not because you're locked in.

What is considered a "routine edit"?

Text changes, swapping out images, adding staff members to an existing page, and blogging updates. Creating entirely new systemic structures may incur a tiny one-off fee.

Who owns the website and assets?

You own your business content, brand, and media. The custom code and template structure belong to Websol and are licensed to you during your active subscription.
